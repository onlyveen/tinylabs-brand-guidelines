01 — Introduction

About Tinylabs

A creative innovation lab built on curiosity, designing playful, intelligent experiences that make experimentation simple and exciting.

About
Who We Are
Tinylabs is a creative innovation lab built on curiosity, where we design playful, intelligent experiences that make experimentation simple and exciting. We exist to turn curiosity into confidence.
Mission
What We Do
To make exploration accessible, engaging, and empowering through thoughtful design and intuitive experiences. We simplify complexity, encourage experimentation, and help ideas grow with clarity and confidence.
Vision
Where We Go
Small ideas spark big futures. We believe innovation begins with curiosity, and Tinylabs exists to nurture that first spark into something meaningful and impactful.

Brand Values

05 — Visual Language

Brand Personality

The Tinylabs visual language is rooted in one narrative: “Organic Play for Growing Minds.” Every design decision reflects this balance of warmth and intelligence.

Organic Warmth
Colors drawn from nature — greens, golds, and earth tones — create warmth far from cold, metallic STEM clichés.
Playful Intelligence
Bold, rounded forms signal approachability. DynaPuff carries personality while Geist keeps content sharp and precise.
Confident Clarity
Every element communicates with intention. Simplicity is a feature — reduce noise and let ideas breathe.
Texture & Pattern
Organic textures and nature-inspired patterns add depth — reinforcing the hands-on exploratory spirit of the brand.
Illustration Style
Characters are rounded, expressive, and optimistic — celebrating discovery and making STEM feel welcoming to every child.
Digital & Web UI
Clean interfaces with generous spacing, rounded corners, and the brand color system guide users with confidence and delight.
